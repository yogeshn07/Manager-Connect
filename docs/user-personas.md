# User Personas

## Overview

Manager Connect serves a single, focused user population: managers within a defined organizational unit. Despite the homogenous role, individuals differ in engagement style, wellness interests, and tech comfort. Three primary personas are defined.

---

## Persona 1: The Connector

**Name:** Priya  
**Role:** Senior Manager, Customer Success  
**Tech Comfort:** High  
**Engagement Style:** Initiator — organizes outings, starts conversations, tags others

### Goals
- Keep the manager group socially active and motivated
- Coordinate weekend outings and fitness events easily
- Recognize teammates publicly for their wins

### Pain Points
- Currently manages all coordination via WhatsApp — messy and informal
- Recognition gets lost in busy company Slack channels
- No way to see who is interested in which activities

### How Manager Connect Helps
- One place for activity planning and RSVP
- Recognition wall gives visibility and permanence to shout-outs
- Interest tags on profiles help her find willing participants

---

## Persona 2: The Participant

**Name:** Arjun  
**Role:** Manager, Engineering  
**Tech Comfort:** Medium  
**Engagement Style:** Responsive — joins what others organize, reacts and comments but rarely initiates

### Goals
- Stay in the loop on group activities without missing anything
- Feel connected to peer managers without requiring extra effort
- Track his wellness and fitness habits with light accountability

### Pain Points
- Misses events because they are spread across different channels
- Wants to participate but rarely initiates activity
- Has no lightweight tool to track wellness habits

### How Manager Connect Helps
- Centralized push notifications mean he never misses an event
- Low-effort RSVP and reaction features match his participation style
- Wellness challenges give lightweight accountability without pressure

---

## Persona 3: The Admin

**Name:** Meera  
**Role:** Manager, People & Culture / Platform Admin  
**Tech Comfort:** High  
**Engagement Style:** Moderator — manages the community, ensures quality and safety

### Goals
- Keep the platform healthy, active, and safe
- Onboard new managers quickly and without friction
- Ensure content quality and community positivity

### Pain Points
- No admin tooling exists in informal channels like WhatsApp
- Cannot see who is engaged versus who has dropped off
- Content moderation is invisible until a problem escalates

### How Manager Connect Helps
- Admin panel for user management, invitations, and engagement visibility
- Invite-only flow gives controlled, auditable access
- Content flagging and moderation tools maintain community standards

---

## Persona Summary

| Attribute | Priya (Connector) | Arjun (Participant) | Meera (Admin) |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|---------------|
| Primary action | Creates and organizes | RSVPs and reacts | Manages and moderates |
| Notification need | Low (she initiates) | High (needs reminders) | Medium (moderation alerts) |
| Key features | Activity creation, recognition | Feed, challenges, RSVP | Admin panel, user management |
| Risk if unserved | Platform feels flat | Platform feels forgettable | Platform becomes unsafe |
